Dog Grooming

What grooming actually includes

  • Bath & dry (including proper drying of dense coats)
  • De-shedding / de-matting (if needed and safe)
  • Trim / clip (breed and coat dependent)
  • Nails, ears, hygiene tidy (as appropriate)

Tip: The best groomers will talk you through what’s realistic for your dog’s coat and comfort.

How to choose a groomer

  • They ask about your dog’s temperament, health, and coat history
  • They’re clear about time, pricing, and what happens if your dog is stressed
  • They show good hygiene and calm handling
  • They’re honest about mats (sometimes clipping is the kindest option)

Questions to ask

  • Do you do one-to-one appointments or multiple dogs at once?
  • How do you handle nervous dogs?
  • Can you work with my dog’s coat type (double coat, doodle, spaniel, etc.)?
  • What aftercare do you recommend?
