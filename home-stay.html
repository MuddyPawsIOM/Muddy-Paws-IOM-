Home Stay (In-home Pet Sitting)

What “good” home sitting looks like

  • Your pet keeps their normal routine in their own home
  • Clear feeding, medication, and walking instructions are followed
  • Regular updates (photos + short notes)
  • Respectful key handling and home security

Before you book: your checklist

  • Meet & greet in your home (always)
  • Emergency contact + vet details shared
  • Written agreement: visits/overnights, times, pricing, cancellations
  • Insurance (and what it covers)
  • What happens if the sitter is unwell?

Keys & safety

  • Use a labelled key with no home address on it
  • Ask how keys are stored and who has access
  • Agree what areas of the home are “off limits” (if any)
