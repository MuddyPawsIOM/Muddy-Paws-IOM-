Dog Walking

What to look for

  • Insurance + clear terms (cancellations, keys, emergencies)
  • Safe transport (if used), ventilation, secured dogs
  • Group size that suits your dog
  • Solid recall / lead policies
  • Updates you actually want (photos, route notes, etc.)

Questions to ask

  • Are dogs walked solo or in groups?
  • Do you do a meet & greet / trial walk?
  • What happens if my dog is injured or becomes unwell?
  • Do you carry water / first aid?
